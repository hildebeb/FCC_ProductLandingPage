About Arrowhil Archery

Small at home custom wood arrow maker. Canadian manufactured wood arrow shafts are used to make these arrows. The woods used to make the shafts are Sitka Spruce and Hemlock from down falled trees on the west coast of British Columbia. Other supplies are currently brought in from 3Rivers Archery. All prep work, staining, sealing, fletching, nocking and gluing on the point be it a field point or broadhead are done in the small home based shop.
